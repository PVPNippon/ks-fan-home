Article 4-1 (Items of information to be acquired, purpose of use, acquisition method)

When using this application and this service, the following information will be automatically acquired via the application for the following purposes.

What personal information do We process?
We collect Your email address to facilitate Your sign-up and login process. Your name is used to display on Your user profile and as an identifier in communications and transactions, such as emails and online payments. Additionally, We use Your profile picture from Your Google account to personalize Your user experience.
If You are accessing Our services as an Administrator, We also process the following additional information:
Third-party authentication information (e.g., YouTube for secondary account access)
Business email address (used as the sender address in burst emails to fans)

Do We process any sensitive personal information?
Our Application integrates with Stripe for payment processing and Shopify for order management, where You may be asked to enter payment details and Your address. However, it’s important to note that Our Application does not store any of this sensitive information directly. The only data We retain in Our system is a unique ID linked to the transactions conducted through Stripe and Shopify, as well as the user’s Account itself, ensuring Your payment details and address remain confidential.

To understand how Your personal information is managed by Shopify and Stripe, please refer to their respective privacy policies available at their websites. [1] [2]

If You are accessing Our Services as an Administrator, We additionally process access to Your Device storage for the file picker, which is used to upload pictures from Your gallery. This access is necessary for managing content that is directly uploaded to the platform through admin functionalities.

Do We receive any information from third parties?
Our Services incorporate data received from third parties to enhance user experience and operational efficiency. This includes limited access to Google Account information, which facilitates certain functionalities within Our Application. Although We do not use or store location data, the integration with Google Maps API may prompt users to grant or deny access to their location data. Additionally, We receive transactional data from Stripe and Shopify, which is essential for processing payments and managing orders respectively. These third-party Services are integral to Our operations, ensuring both the functionality of Our Services and the security of user data, while adhering to strict privacy standards.
For administrators, additional information is processed: Authentication tokens from YouTube and Device tokens from the Firestore database. These tokens enhance administrative capabilities within the app, allowing for effective management of content and user interactions on a more granular level.

How do We process Your information?
Our Application securely processes user information to improve Service quality and ensure data security. We use Google’s native authentication libraries for secure authentication, Shopify for order fulfillment, Stripe for payment processing, and Sendgrid as a trusted third party for sending emails to users. User data, i.e., email addresses and Device tokens (excluding addresses), is securely transmitted via trusted third-party channels and stored in Our backend database to facilitate transactions and push notifications.
We only retain minimal information necessary for tracking orders from Our Shopify-powered in-app store, maintaining high standards of privacy and efficiency. However, for transparency, please note that billing and shipping addresses are visible to Us as merchants in the dashboard to facilitate order processing and customer Service interactions.
For administrators, We additionally process YouTube tokens to enable effective video content management and uploading within the app.

In what situations and with which parties do We share personal information?
We share Your Personal information with third parties in specific situations to ensure the proper functioning and efficiency of Our Services. These include:
Shopify: We do not share any information directly with Shopify. However, We leverage their platform for order fulfillment. The user’s email and home or billing address information is available to Us through the merchant Account on Shopify.
Stripe: Our access to Stripe is limited to invoice references and basic payment details. We receive the last four digits of the card number, the card type (e.g., Visa, Mastercard), a simple reference number, and binary data indicating whether a payment was successful. Additionally, the user’s email address is shared with Stripe to facilitate transaction processing.
Sendgrid: The user’s email address is shared with Sendgrid Services to fulfill Our email sending requirements. Sendgrid is a trusted third-party Service Provider that helps Us manage Our email communications with users.

How do We keep Your information safe?
We use Google OAuth 2.0 [4] for secure user authentication and rely on trusted third-party providers like Stripe for payment processing, Shopify for order fulfillment, and Sendgrid for email communications. These Services are known for their robust security measures, ensuring that Your Personal information is handled securely and in accordance with industry standards. Stripe, in particular, is used for securely processing payment details, and We only access masked payment information such as the last four digits of the card number and card type.

How We Share Your Information

Google Account Information:
Our Services integrate with Google Services such as Google Maps API, Google OAuth, and Google Cloud to enhance functionality. For general users, We may receive access to certain information from Your Google Account, with access strictly limited to what is necessary for the Service. You will be informed of the specific permissions requested when You link Your Google Account. For admin users, additional permissions may be required to manage media uploads. These activities are supported by Our backend systems, primarily using Google Firestore, and the data can be accessed by all users as per the intended functionality of the app.
Location Data:
While Our Services do not use or store Your location data, the use of Google Maps API may require a request for access to Your location. This request is generated solely by Google Maps and not by Our Application. You have the option to allow or deny this request. If granted, We do not store or utilize Your location data, as it is not necessary for Our Service offering. For admin users, no additional location data is used beyond what is required for general users.
Admin-Specific Data Usage:
Admin users have enhanced capabilities within the app, including posting updates, uploading media, sending push notifications, and creating events. Data related to these activities is sent to Our backend database and is accessible to all users of the app. This is essential for the functionality of Our Services and is designed to support the interactive features of the app. Admins are informed of these data Usage practices when they assume their roles and responsibilities.
Sendgrid Usage:
We use Sendgrid Services to fulfill Our email sending requirements. Reports about the sender and receiver, including user email addresses, are stored in the Sendgrid dashboard. Sendgrid is a trusted third-party Service Provider. For more details on how they handle data, please refer to their privacy policy: Sendgrid Privacy Policy [3].
Shopify Usage:
User addresses (home or billing address) are entered into Shopify forms for order fulfillment. This information is hosted at the Shopify merchant Account and is visible to the merchant to facilitate order processing and customer Service interactions.
Stripe Usage:
Our access to Stripe is limited to invoice references and basic payment details. We receive the last four digits of the card number, the card type (e.g., Visa, Mastercard), a simple reference number, and binary data indicating whether a payment was successful. Additionally, the user’s email address is shared with Stripe to facilitate transaction processing, including managing auto-renewal for subscriptions. Users can disable auto-renewal through Account settings.

In all instances, We ensure that any information received from third parties is handled in compliance with applicable privacy laws and Our privacy policy.

Security of Your Personal Data
The security of Your Personal Data is important to Us. We use Firestore’s server-side encryption to safeguard Your data. Firestore encrypts all data before it is written to disk and decrypts it when read from disk, ensuring that Your data remains protected at all times. For more details, You can refer to Firestore’s documentation on server-side encryption [5].
While We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect Your Personal Data, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, We cannot guarantee its absolute security.
